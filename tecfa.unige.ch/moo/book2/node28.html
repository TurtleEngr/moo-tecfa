Adding the Channel System

 

First check, if you don't already ``have'' channels by typing: @features. If you see the #3619 channel feature like this: 

     >@features
     Feature Name
     ------- ----
     #225    xy's improved social FO
              [.......]
     #3619   channel feature
     ------- ----
     10 features found.

you don't need to read further. If the Channel feature is missing on your character, you do this: 

    @add-feature &channel_feature

Casual users probably want to use just existing channels and don't need to read further but should remember the 'help &channel_feature' command. Actually, 'help <most anything>' will most of the time bring you a step further in the MOO.
