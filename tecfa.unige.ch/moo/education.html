Moo Education at Tecfa & with Tecfa

At Tecfa

Students in our postgraduate "STAF"diploma in educational technology (french documentation) get exposure to Moos.

  • Tecfa STAF students have to follow a basic computer training sessions before courses start (includes how to use Internet Services (www navigation, basic html, moo navigation and communication). A french description is available .
  • Systèmes d'Information et Communication Médiatisés par Ordinateur (STAF-14): Students are first taught basic Moo navigation and building. Then, they receive an introduction to programming with the help of some simple MOO programming exercises: see the "Introduction to MOO Programming" chapter in the TecfaMOO technical manual. Finally Ken's Tutoring Bot (see the manual above) is used for teaching regular expressions.
  • See also: TecfaMOO Student Projects
  • Bases techniques des espaces virtuels (STAF-18): Spring 1997 (a class on virtual environments, were students can optionally use MOO technology)
  • STAF-2x Only in fall 95: A optional 2nd year programming course. ( 1/4 Moo, 1/4 Python, 1/4 Javascript, 1/4 VRML). Content has been transferred to Staf-14 and STAF-18.
