TecfaMOO and the MOO community

One of the goals of the TecfaMOO projects is to increase academic recognition for this technology. [more to come]

Services offered:

  • Tecfa's Educational MUD page, part of the Educational Technology WWW VL page.
  • MUD related things in TECFA's FTP directory (we put a copy of most manuals and FAQs here... but you won't always get the lastet versions): Also available via: ftp tecfa.unige.ch pub/documentation/MUD.
  • Copies of some MOO Manuals (e.g. the MOO Programmer's Manual).
  • The TecfaMOO Technical MOO Manaul. Has some sections of general interest, e.g. a MOO Programming Tutorial. Quality is not great, but there isn't much documentation about MOOing anyhow ;) ... updated on an irregular basis.
  • The SPIDER Bibliography (Under Construction)

Other things

  • Educational MOO Administrators forum

Sponsoring:

  • E_MOO, dedicated to research and development in MUD and MOO technology is hosted on a Tecfa machine. E_MOO is state of the art moo technology !
