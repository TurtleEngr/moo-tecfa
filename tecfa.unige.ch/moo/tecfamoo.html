The TecfaMOO

The TECFAMOO is a text-based virtual reality. It is a Virtual Space for Educational Technology, Education, Research and Life at TECFA, School of Psychology and Education, University of Geneva, Switzerland.

The TECFAMOO project has been started as a small informal project in the fall of 1994. We have been using MOO technology as internal CMC tool (most Tecfa Members are "on the MOO"), for some teaching and some research.
